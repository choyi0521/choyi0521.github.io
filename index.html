About Me

I obtained Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Mathematical Science from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I am currently a MS student at KAIST, advised by Jaegul Choo. My research interests lie in the areas of machine learning and computer vision. Especially, my recent studies focus on time series forecasting and image generation with minimal supervision. I enjoy studying interesting algorithms and mathematical theories, and my research interests are often motivated by them.
